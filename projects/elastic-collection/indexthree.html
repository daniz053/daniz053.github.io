About Our Project
This project was completed through Parson’s Core Interaction 2 creative coding class. We were asked to collect, or create, a collection of 50 to 100 items. Our collections needed to include at least five different data type affiliated with it. Additionally, we required to have a visual consistency between the items collected. From there, we were asked to trade our collections with an assigned classmate. Through this, we designed a website for our partner’s collection connected to the API. Through this experience, our goal was to make use of an API to design, and build an engaging and cohesive data visual.
