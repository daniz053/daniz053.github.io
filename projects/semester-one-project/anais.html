ENTRY SEVEN

When thinking of home a lot of the visuals that pop up in my head involve nature, my neighborhood up in the Bronx has a lot of plants and trees and wildlife.

I tend to feel most nostalgic for the springtime when we start to get a lot of blooming flowers and shade from the trees and for some reason my mind connects the idea of home that moment of rebirth in nature.I feel most comfort in thinking about the birds and butterflies and bees that come out when the temperature becomes warm again.

I think the idea of warmth and growing warmth is why I associate home with nature.
