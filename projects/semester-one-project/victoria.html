ENTRY NINE

My home is a safe space for me. Time can change and I can love how my life in New York is but there is a gap i feel that gets fulfilled every time im home. I think its because it is a different country and I really miss the warmth of people and the food I grew up with.

My house is big and red and filled with nature, most of my memories come from walking to our local park and doing trails and smelling the smell of humid rainforest. I love going back home, but I also know at 21 I need to experience something different than home in order to grow. I know home will always be there for me.
