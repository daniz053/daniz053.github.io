ENTRY EIGHT

The place I feel most at home is with a good cup of warm coffee and reading a book. It's been a long time since I've been able to just have that moment to myself but when I take the space to breathe and just be, it soothes me.

I'll sit in a warm space, blanket optional, facing the sun. If the weather permits, I'll even sit outside. The silence of it all quiets my mind and really helps me check in with myself. That is home for me.
